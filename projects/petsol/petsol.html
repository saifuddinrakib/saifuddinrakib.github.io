PetSol (Pet Solution)

Overview

PetSol (Pet Solution) is an award-winning IoT-based automated bird care system designed to provide comprehensive care and monitoring for pet birds. This innovative project combines hardware automation with mobile application integration to ensure the well-being of birds through intelligent monitoring and automated care features.

PetSol represents a perfect blend of mechanical engineering, electronics, and software development, creating a holistic solution for modern pet bird care that addresses all essential needs from feeding to security.

Key Functionalities

  • Automated Watering System: Dedicated water chamber with servo motor-controlled mechanism for automatic water dispensing. Ensures birds have constant access to fresh water with intelligent level monitoring.
  • Automated Feeding System: Smart food chamber with servo motor for scheduled feeding. The container can be locked and unlocked remotely, ensuring portion control and timely feeding.
  • Air Quality Control: Integrated air quality sensors monitor the environment inside the cage, ensuring optimal breathing conditions for birds with automatic ventilation adjustments.
  • Temperature Monitoring & Control: Real-time temperature monitoring with automatic climate control to maintain ideal temperature ranges for bird health and comfort.
  • Emergency Security Sensor: Advanced sensor system detects potential threats from harmful animals or intruders, triggering instant alerts to protect the birds.
  • Automated Cleaning System: Innovative triangular-shaped wiper with servo motor attached to the lower dust deck that automatically cleans accumulated dust and debris at scheduled intervals.
  • Mobile Application Monitoring: Comprehensive mobile app for real-time monitoring of all system parameters. Receives instant push notifications when food or water chambers are empty or when any emergency situation is detected.

Technical Components

Hardware Architecture:

  • Arduino Motherboard: Central control unit for all system operations.
  • Multiple Sensors: Air quality, temperature, motion detection, and level sensors.
  • 3 Servo Motors: Controls for food container lock/unlock, water dispenser, and automated wiper for dust cleaning.
  • Power Management System: Efficient power distribution and backup.
  • Wireless Communication Module: For mobile app connectivity.
